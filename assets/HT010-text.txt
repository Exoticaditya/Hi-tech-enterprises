photoelectric registration optional.🔌 Mechanical & PowerMain Power Supply:• Typically 220 – 440 V AC, 50/60 Hz, three-phase (Some models use single phase for lighter machines).Power Consumption: Approx 2 – 8 kW depending on machine size and optional attachments.Compressed Air: ~5–8 CFM @ ~6 bar (6 kg/cm²) for pneumatic operations.🏗️ Construction & BuildFrame: Rugged Mild Steel or Stainless Steel (SS) construction for hygiene and corrosion resistance.Product Contact Parts: Typically SS or better for food/chemical applications.Safety: Emergency stops, guarding and interlocks generally included.📏 Dimensions & WeightMachine Weight: From ~500 kg up to ~1 000 kg+ depending on configuration and filler type (single vs multi-head).Footprint: Varies by model — common sizes around 1100 mm × 1000 mm × 2500 mm (L×W×H).🔧 Optional FeaturesMulti-head weighers (4/8/10 head) for higher speed & accuracy.Servo auger filler for fine powders.Infeed/outfeed conveyors.Batch/Date coding, nitrogen flushing 📌 Common Applications✔ Granules (snacks, pulses, sugar, rice)✔ Powdered products (spices, flour, detergent)✔ Non-free-flow products with auger or multi-head weighers✔ FMCG, food & beverage, chemical, agricultural sectors
