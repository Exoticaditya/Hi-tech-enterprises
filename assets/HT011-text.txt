Automatic fully pneumatic cup filler packing machine ( HT - 011)Technical Specifications — Fully Pneumatic Cup Filler Pouch Packing Machine🔧 Filling SystemType: Volumetric cup filler with interchangeable cups for different weights. Filling Mechanism: Semi-automatic — pneumatic assist for pouch feed/closing and manual/servo cup actuation. Accuracy: Approximately ±0.2 % to ±4 % depending on product and calibration. 🧠 Control & ElectronicsControl System: PLC-based control with HMI touchscreen for easy parameter setup.Sealing System: Pneumatic or servo actuated vertical & horizontal heat sealing.Temperature Control: PID digital temperature controllers for accurate sealing.Film Pulling & Registration: Servo/AC drive or belt pull system; photoelectric registration optional.Control System: Typically PLC or electronic controller for sealing and timing; basic machines may use simple logic controls. Sensors: Photoelectric eye/registration sensor for accurate pouch cut & placement (in many models). 📊 Filling & Product RangeFilling Range (Weight):• Typical ranges: 100g – 200g ,500 g – 1000 g  (interchangeable cups). Product Types: Free-flow items like sugar, salt, tea, pulses, coffee, spices, granules, small snacks, detergent powder, etc. 🕐 PerformanceSpeed: ~18 – 28 pouches/min depending on product, pouch size, and operator control. Sealing Types: Center seal or three-side seal configurations. 📐 Dimensions & WeightMachine Footprint: Commonly around ~1150 mm x 1000 mm x 2200 mm (L×W×H) (varies by model). Net Weight: Approx 400 – 600 kg. ⚡ Power & PneumaticPower Supply: Often 220 V AC, single phase, 50 Hz (some industrial models multi-phase Three Phace). Power Consumption: Around 2.5 kW typical (varies). Compressed Air: ~6 CFM @ ~6 bar (6 kg/cm²) for pneumatic operations. 🛠️ ConstructionFrame: Mild steel body with stainless steel (SS) product contact parts. Hopper & Cup Filling Components: Food-grade SS or compatible materials. 📦 Film & Pouch DetailsFilm Material: Heat-sealable laminated film rolls.Film Width & Pouch Size: Typically customizable based on product — up to ~450 mm film width. 🧩 Optional FeaturesBatch coding/printing unitNitrogen flushing for extended shelf-life packagingAdjustable cup sets for varied fill weights 📌 Typical Applications✔ Snacks & small granules (e.g., kurkure, puffs)✔ Tea, coffee, sugar, spices & pulses✔ Free-flow powders (e.g., detergents)✔ Seeds, nuts & similar products requiring cup dosing 📍 How It Works (Quick Overview)Film Roll Unwinds: Film is fed from a roll and shaped into a pouch. Filling: Volumetric cup fills product; operator or pneumatic trigger releases product into pouch. Sealing: Pneumatic heat seals pouch (center or 3-side). Cut & Output: Completed pouch is cut and discharged. //
