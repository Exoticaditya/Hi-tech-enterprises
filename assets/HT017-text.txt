Manual Pneumatic Sealing Machine ( HT -017) Technical SpecificationsOperation: Pneumatic (compressed air-driven jaw movement) for consistent pressure and seal quality. Power Supply: 220–240 V AC, single phase, 50 Hz (varies by region/model). Compressed Air Requirement: Typically 4–8 kg/cm² (≈ 0.4–0.8 MPa) depending on seal bar size and material thickness. Sealing SpecificationsSeal Length: Common ranges 150 mm to 400 mm (smaller benchtop to larger industrial machines). Seal Width: Usually 3 mm – 15 mm (standard ~10 mm for most packaging). Sealing Method: Heat Sealing — for heat-seal films Direct Heat Sealing — for thicker laminated, foil, or multi-layer materials. Controls & AdjustmentDigital Timer / Temperature Controller — to adjust heat and dwell time precisely. Adjustable Pneumatic Pressure Control — for different film thickness and materials. Pedal or Manual Start — operator controls sealing action. Performance & CapacityProduction Rate: ~1,000 – 10,000 pouches/day in  models; rate depending on operator and pouch size. Pack Weight Range: Handles pouches containing ~.500 kg–5 kg (varies by machine and film). Construction & MaterialsBody Material: Mild Steel (MS) with powder coating or SS  for hygienic applications. Sealing Jaw Type: Often scissor-type pneumatic jaws giving uniform pressure across the seal. L X W X H           ( 610 X 720 X 750 MM )📌 Manual Pneumatic Sealer MachineSpecificationTypical ValueModel ExamplePneumatic  SealerSeal Length150 mm – 500 mm Seal Width3 mm / 5 mm / 8 mm / 10 mm Power Supply230 V AC, 50 Hz Sealing TypeImpulse / Direct Heat Compressed Air Pressure~6–8 kg/cm² ConstructionMS body with powder coat (SS optional) ControlsDigital timer / temperature controller Capacity~1000–10 000 bags/day (varies) Pedal or Manual Start       operator controls sealing actionL X W X H           ( 610 X 720 X 750 mm) //
