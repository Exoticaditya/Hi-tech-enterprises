HT-010

Collar Type Multihead Weigher

Automatic collar type vertical form-fill-seal (VFFS) packaging machine with multihead weigher. Ideal for high-speed precision packing of chips, snacks, namkeen, grains, pulses, and dry fruits.

General Machine Type: Collar type vertical form-fill-seal (VFFS) packaging machine with weigh filling system.

Filling System: Weigh filler (single/dual/multi-head electronic weighers) or auger filler depending on product type (granules or powders).

Performance & Capacity: Typical filling range from 10g to 1kg, with extended options up to 2kg+. Packing speed ranges from 15 to 55 bags/min with high weighing accuracy (±1% to ±3%).

Bag Specifications: Supports center-seal pillow pouches with widths up to 400mm and lengths up to 500mm.

Control & Electronics: PLC-based control with HMI touchscreen, PID digital temperature controllers, and servo/AC drive film pulling system.

Construction: rugged Mild Steel or Stainless Steel (SS) construction for hygiene and safety features included.

Key Specifications

  • Filling Range: 10g - 1000g (up to 2kg optional)
  • Speed: 15 – 55 bags/min
  • Accuracy: ±1% – ±3%
  • Bag Width: 50mm - 400mm
  • Power: 2kW - 8kW
